Target Audience

Audience goes from males to females, adults, teens and families to which weather forecast could be relevant, like local drivers, farmers, young adventurous and travelers in general. The audience is middle to high-income class who are interested in quality weather forecasting. The purpose is help them to have a break from stressful lives (work, business, school) and let them try something new that will bring some healthy adrenaline rush. Because the audience vary significantly, the website should able to be accessed from multiple platforms: desktop, laptop, tablets and mobile smartphones.
